About Us

D P Air-condiitoning since the past two decades is a leading service provider dealing into various air conditioning solutions. Our clients are from various sectors as in the corporate, government and consumer. Our constant efforts are towards research and innovation in the field of air conditioning.

Currently, one of our product is in the market labelled as “Aeroseat.” Aeroseat is a comfortable sofa which has features like regulated air flow and humid control. This sofa controls the surrounding temperature by a drop of 25 degrees then the ambient temperature. We have this product available into various variants depending upon the purpose of the event.

The product is being manufactured by associated vendors and channel partners and assembled in-house. With innovation in it’s products, unique applications, potential investments and financial supports, D. P. Air is seeking exponential success of Aeroseat in India and the global market.
